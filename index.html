SaaS Startup (Auto Repair App)

As an entrepreneur, I founded a SaaS startup, developing an innovative auto repair platform that allows car owners to find repair shops and request services online. Key features of the platform include:

  • Real-time car diagnostics and maintenance scheduling.
  • Cost estimation tool and in-app payments.
  • Mobile mechanic service, towing integration, and insurance integration.
  • Subscription-based for repair shops.
  • Chatbot integration for customer service.

My role involved product management, overseeing the technical development of the platform, and implementing a scalable infrastructure on AWS to support the app's growth.
